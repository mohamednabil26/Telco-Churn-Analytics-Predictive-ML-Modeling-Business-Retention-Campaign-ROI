# Telco Churn Analytics — Predictive ML Modeling & Retention Campaign ROI

Customer churn is one of the biggest financial leaks in the telecommunications industry. Acquiring a new customer costs significantly more than retaining an existing one. Without automated, predictive insights, companies struggle to identify at-risk users in time, leading to massive revenue losses and inefficient marketing spend.

To solve this business challenge, we built an end-to-end Machine Learning pipeline that accurately predicts customer churn patterns. Beyond standard accuracy metrics, this project includes a financial simulation framework that directly translates model predictions into business value, calculating potential revenue saved and the Return on Investment (ROI) of targeted retention campaigns.

What makes this project unique is its direct bridge between Data Science and Business Value. It doesn't just output charts; it simulates a real-world $15,000 marketing campaign to show how a 25% retention success rate dynamically maximizes net profit.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Machine Learning Frameworks:** Scikit-Learn (Logistic Regression, Random Forest Classifier)
* **Data Preprocessing & Encoding:** LabelEncoder, Categorical Feature Engineering, and Missing Value Handling
* **Data Analysis & Visualization:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn (Comprehensive Cohort & Feature Evaluation)
* **Business Intelligence Simulation:** Dynamic Financial Modeling (Campaign Cost, Revenue Saved, Net Profit, and ROI Analytics)
